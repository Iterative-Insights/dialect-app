### Visual Design Elements

- **Typography**: Use a clean, readable font like Arial or Helvetica.
- **Color Scheme**: A simple color palette with a primary color for buttons and headers. Use contrasting colors for text and background for readability.
- **Whitespace**: Ample spacing around elements to avoid clutter and enhance readability.
- **Imagery**: Minimal use of images. If necessary, use high-quality, relevant images in the header or section backgrounds.

This basic outline provides a starting point. You can further customize it with your branding elements, such as a logo, specific color palette, and any additional styling or interactivity you wish to include.
